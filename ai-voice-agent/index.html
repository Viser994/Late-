How it works

Live on your existing number in three steps

No new hardware. No new number to advertise. It plugs into the phone line you already have.

1

Forward missed calls

We set your line to forward calls that go unanswered (or all calls after hours) to your Answerly number. Takes minutes with your carrier.

2

The AI answers & talks

Answerly greets the caller in a natural voice, understands what they need, answers questions, and books appointments straight into your calendar.

3

You get the lead instantly

The moment the call ends, you get a text and email with the caller’s name, number, reason, and a full transcript. Nothing slips through.

FAQ

Questions, answered

Do I need a new phone number?

No. Answerly works with the number you already have. You simply forward unanswered or after-hours calls to your dedicated Answerly number — your customers keep dialing the same line.

Will callers know it’s an AI?

The voice is natural and conversational. You choose whether it introduces itself as a virtual assistant. Most businesses do, and customers are happy as long as they get help fast.

Can it actually book appointments?

Yes. On the Professional plan it connects to Google Calendar, Calendly, or your booking system, checks real availability, and confirms the appointment on the call.

What happens for something the AI can’t handle?

It gracefully takes a detailed message, or on eligible plans warm-transfers to a staff member. You’re always notified instantly with the full context.

How fast can I go live?

Most businesses are live the same day. We build your script, train it on your services, provision your number, and help you set up call forwarding.

Is this the demo I’m using now?

The demo above runs on your browser’s speech engine so you can try it with zero setup. Production uses a real phone number, premium voices, and a live LLM — see the technical reference in the project’s server/ folder.
